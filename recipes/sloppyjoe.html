Sloppy Joe Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 16oz Ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1/8 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 8 pack of hamburger buns

Instructions

  1. Brown the beef in a pan and make sure to ground it up.
  2. After the meat is cooked, add ketchup, mustard, brown sugar, and vinegar, in that order
  3. Stir the mixture into a even consistancy, set on low heat
  4. Once it is simmering remove from the heat and serve on buns.
